# Privacy Policy

**Dragon Hoard - Game Collection Tracker**

Last updated: February 20, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Dragon Hoard ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Dragon Hoard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using Dragon Hoard, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- **Email address**: Required for account creation and login (when using email sign-in)
- **Apple ID**: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email (or a relay address if you choose to hide it)
- **Guest accounts**: You may use the App without providing personal information. Guest accounts are anonymous and linked only to your device.

### 2.2 Game Collection Data
- Game titles, platforms, and status (backlog, playing, completed)
- Purchase prices and estimated market values
- Game condition, region, and quantity
- Personal notes and ratings
- Wishlist items

### 2.3 Camera and Photos
- When using the Shelf Scanner feature, the App accesses your camera to photograph game shelves
- Photos are sent to Google Gemini AI for game identification and are **not stored** on our servers
- Photos are processed in memory only and discarded immediately after identification

### 2.4 Usage Data
- App feature usage statistics (only with your consent)
- Savings tracking data
- Achievement and badge progress
- Daily streak and XP progression

### 2.5 Technical Information
- Device type and operating system (for app compatibility)
- App version
- Device locale and region (for currency and language settings)

**Note:** With your explicit consent, we may collect anonymized crash reports and usage analytics to improve the App (see Section 4.5). You can enable or disable this at any time in the App settings.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Sync your game collection across devices
- Calculate your collection's estimated market value based on public sales data
- Track your savings and gaming goals
- Send important service notifications
- Improve the App based on usage patterns (with consent)

We **do not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Dragon Hoard uses the following third-party services:

### 4.1 Supabase (Database & Authentication)
- Stores your account and collection data on secure servers
- Handles authentication (email/password and anonymous sign-in)
- Data is protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your data
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### 4.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
- Manages premium subscriptions and in-app purchases
- Receives your anonymous user ID and subscription status
- Does not receive your email, name, or game collection data
- Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### 4.3 Google Gemini AI (Shelf Scanner)
- Processes photos to identify games on your shelves
- Images are sent via our secure server (Supabase Edge Function), not directly from your device
- Images are processed in real-time and **not stored** by us or Google beyond the request
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 4.4 IGDB (Game Database)
- Provides game information, cover images, and metadata
- Only game search queries are sent — no personal data
- Privacy Policy: https://www.igdb.com/privacy_policy

### 4.5 Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)
- Collects anonymized usage statistics and crash reports (**only with your consent**)
- No personal data is shared — only aggregated metrics
- You can opt out at any time in the App settings
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 4.6 Jina.ai & eBay (Price Estimation)
- Used to estimate game market values from publicly available eBay sold listings
- Jina.ai reads public eBay search result pages on our behalf
- Only game titles and platform names are sent — **no personal data**
- Price estimates are approximate and based on recent completed sales
- Privacy Policy (Jina): https://jina.ai/legal/privacy-policy

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Sensitive local data (subscription status, progress) is stored in your device's encrypted Keychain
- Non-sensitive preferences are stored locally on your device
- We implement industry-standard security measures

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data (available in App settings)
- **Export**: Export your game collection data (PDF export feature)

Account deletion permanently removes all your data from our servers and clears all locally stored data from your device.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at the email below.

---

## 7. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted immediately upon request
- Anonymous crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days

---

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence. Supabase servers may be located in the EU or US. All transfers are protected by HTTPS encryption and comply with applicable data protection regulations.
